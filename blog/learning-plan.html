Learning Plan
What is your long term goal or career pathway?
My goal is to find a career in Web Development or Software Development. I wanted to get into the programming space to begin with, then see what avenues there are to pursue and grow in. I came into this not knowing much about the space, but I started with web development as I feel this can have relevance to my current workplace and opportunities within it.
A description of your strengths and limitations when it comes to learning
Strengths:
  • Hands on learner - I grasp concepts quite quickly when I am able to actively practice them while I learn.
  • Passion for knowledge - I love knowing things and how they are relevant to my life or others around me
  • Curious - I ask lots of questions about how things work and push myself to understand them
  • Hard-working - I push myself to the limits to complete a job or exercise. I do not like to leave something incomplete.
Weaknesses:
  • Easily distracted - I get side tracked quite easily if I find a topic or a point that I want to know more about.
  • Communication - I struggle to communicate things succinctly. My thought processes are not always the most efficient which can lead to explanations missing the mark.
  • Can feel smothered - I can get overwhelmed when I feel there is too much to do. It can lead to me being extremely unproductive if I do not manage it well.
  • Overthinker - I often overthink concepts or a solution leading to my final solution being over-engineered.
What skills (non-technical/human skills) would you like to see developed in yourself while at Dev Academy?
I would love to develop my communication and time management. These are a couple of the most important skills to have in all aspects of life and I think I only stand to gain by working on them.

  • I have a workspace that allows me to confine myself if I need some a focussed environment.

What you will do when things are building up and starting to get to you?
  • Take a breather. Take 5-15 minutes to go and get a drink, fresh air or just be on my feet away from a screen.
  • Reset. Take 10 minutes to reassess where I am at and how I can get back on track.
  • Reach out. Talk to my classmates, facilitators or my family and friends to get a different perspective on my situation.
A commitment as to how and where you will seek help in a timely way
I will give myself a half hour to try and fix something myself. After that, I will reach out on discord to get another perspective. I will be as specific as possible, providing as much context and material as I can.
